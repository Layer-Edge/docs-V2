# edgen Staking

## Introduction

Staking EDGEN allows users to earn protocol rewards, participate in LayerEdge's verification economy, and access ecosystem incentives.

LayerEdge supports two staking modes: Tiered Staking for committed participants, and Normal Staking for smaller holders. Both modes are non-custodial and integrated directly into the protocol.

here : https://layeredge.foundation/staking


## Staking EDGEN (Tiered Staking)

Tiered Staking offers enhanced APYs for users staking ≥ 3,000 EDGEN.

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Go to the LayerEdge Staking Page and click **Connect Wallet**.

### Step 3: Understand Tiered Staking System

* **Tier 3** → Remaining 50% → 20% APY

### Important Notes

Once staked, your wallet enters the tier system permanently. Unstaking will **demote you to Tier 3**, and rejoining higher tiers will require a **new wallet**.
